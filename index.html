🏴‍☠️ Captain's Log

To those brave enough to seek the lost Midsummer Treasure,

The sea has been kind to no sailor, and many have searched for my fortune. Yet the clever and the bold may still uncover what remains hidden.

The first piece of the map was lost when a storm drove our ship upon the rocks. The second was hidden where laughter is loudest and thirsty sailors gather after their work is done.

Look for the place where food and drink bring the crew together. There you shall find the next clue.

— Captain Blackbeard
